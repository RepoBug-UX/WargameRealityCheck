Wargame audits

A tool that takes a wargame as input, extracts its implicit probability assumptions, and audits each one against live prediction-market prices from Polymarket and Kalshi. Each audit produces three first-class output components: strict disagreements, structured comparisons (where the wargame's conditional probability and the market's unconditional price measure different things), and in-document tensions surfaced from the wargame's own narrative.
